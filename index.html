Meet Kate!

Hello! I'm Kate Reed, and I absolutely love bringing animated characters to life! For over 10 years, I've been the voice behind your favorite cartoon friends, from brave heroes to silly sidekicks.

Whether it's a bubbly princess, a mischievous monster, or a wise old wizard, I create unique voices that help tell amazing stories. I work with talented animators and directors to make sure every character feels real and special.

When I'm not in the recording booth, I love spending time with my own kids, reading stories, and finding inspiration for new character voices everywhere I go!
